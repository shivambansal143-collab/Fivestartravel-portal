## Cost Drivers in Travel Technology

Large B2B travel systems are expensive to build because they require:

- massive search infrastructure
- complex booking logic
- multi-supplier integrations
- real-time pricing engines
- high scalability

### Typical Build Range

Enterprise-grade travel platforms often range from **$500k to $5M** depending on integrations, geography, compliance, and scale.
